About Me

Hi! I'm Michael, a former high school English teacher and current employee of Generation Esports--the parent company of Gaming Concepts and Blueprint.

In my 3.5 years at Generation Esports, I have come to appreciate my colleagues' work on our competition platform and especially our learning management system (LMS), Blueprint.

This appreciation turned into geniune interest to learn how to develop web applications like the ones my company operates. I decided to sign up for Codecademy and begin their Full-Stack Engineer course. My desire is to one day join the development team at my company and hopefully focus my development efforts on our Blueprint LMS.
